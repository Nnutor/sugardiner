Carrot Cake

This Easy Carrot Cake Recipe is everything you expect out of carrot cake,This carrot cake requires just 25 minutes of hands-on time. Then all that’s left is baking, cooling, and frosting!Combine the wet ingredients: Beat the butter until smooth, then add in the oil and sugar. Beat for a couple of minutes, then mix in the eggs, vanilla, and buttermilk.

Bake: Divide the batter evenly between three 8″ round cake pans that have been lightly greased and dusted with flour. Bake until a toothpick comes out clean, then cool completely.

Make the frosting: Use an electric mixer to beat the cream cheese until smooth. Add in the powdered sugar and mix, followed by the cream and vanilla. Turn the mixer on high and beat until thick and fluffy.

Frost the cake: Divide the frosting between the cake layers to frost and assemble.

Shrimp Stir-fry

uicy shrimp are sautéed with a gorgeous rainbow of vegetables and a garlic sauce in this easy, fast, delicious garlic shrimp stir-fry.

Sautéed quickly over high heat, with a flavorful sauce and some veggies, Make the garlic sauce. Whisk the sauce ingredients in a small bowl.

Cook the veggies + shrimp. Heat the oil over medium-high heat in a large skillet. Cook, until vegetables begin to soften, 3-4 minutes, then add the shrimp.

Pour the sauce into the skillet. Cook, stirring occasionally, until the sauce is thick enough to coat the shrimp, about 5 minutes.

Serve with steaming rice, cilantro and sesame seeds.

It’s healthy and quick, and it’s easy to make it gluten-free so it accommodates a lot of dietary preferences

The garlic sauce would also be tasty for dipping Steamed Shrimp if you’re looking for a simple snack.

Add some spice: I like to include just a small pinch of red pepper flakes for a little extra flavor. Feel free to add more to turn up the heat!

Enjoy your meal
